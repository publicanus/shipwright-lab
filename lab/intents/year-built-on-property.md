# Record and show the year a property was built

As a broker, buyers keep asking how old a house is — it drives renovation expectations,
energy-label conversations, and price negotiations — and today I have nowhere to put that
answer in Dreamhouse. I want to record the construction year when I list a property and
have it show on the property's record page alongside the other property details.

Everyone who works with properties in Dreamhouse should be able to see the year; the people
who can edit a property today should be able to edit it. A property listed before we started
recording this simply shows no year. Construction years are plain years (e.g. 1987), not
dates — nobody knows or cares about the day a house was finished.
