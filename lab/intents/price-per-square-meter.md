# Show price per square meter on properties and sort by it

As a house hunter comparing properties of very different sizes, the listed price alone
doesn't tell me whether a property is expensive — a small apartment at €400k can be a worse
deal than a large house at €500k. I want to see what a property costs per square meter, and
I want to sort the property list by it so the best-value properties surface first.

Today Dreamhouse doesn't record a property's living area at all, so this needs the area to
be captured on the property first. Brokers enter the area when they list the property; it
should be visible wherever the property's other key figures (price, beds, baths) are shown.
The price per square meter itself should never be hand-entered — it follows from price and
area. Properties without an area recorded simply don't show a price per square meter and
sort after those that do.
